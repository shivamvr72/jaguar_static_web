60 ILLUSTRIOUS YEARS

In March 1961, Jaguar E‑type created history when the hand-built ‘77RW’ was driven from Coventry to Geneva
to be launched alongside the ‘9600HP’ at the Motor Show. Never before had a car engendered
such feelings of passion and British pride.

60 years on, Jaguar Classic celebrates the unveiling of an icon with six matched pairs of ‘9600HP’ and
‘77RW’ - inspired E‑type restorations, including an exclusive Coventry to Geneva driving experience for
their new owners.
